The security measures used to protect data

1 - Awareness, training and education
2 - Trechnology
3 - Policy and procedure

Awareness, training and education are the measures put in place by an organization to ensure that users are knowledgeable about potential security threats and the actions the can take to protect information systems.

Technology refers to the software- and hardware-based solutions designed to protect information systems such as firewalls, wich continously monitor your network in search of possible malicious incidents.

Policy and procedure refers to the administrative controls that provide a foundation for how an organization implements information assurance, such as incident response plans and best practice guidelines.
