[[The World of Cybersecurity.md]]
Cybersecurity is the ongoing effort to protect individuals, organizations and governments from digital attacks by protecting networked systems and data from unauthorized use or harm.
Personal: safeguard your identity, your data and your computing devices.
Organizational: everyone's responsibility to protect the organization's reputation, data and constumers.
Government: protection is even more vital at this level, where national security, economic stability and the safety and wellbeing of citizens are at stake.
